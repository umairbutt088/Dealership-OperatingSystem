MOT Tracker
🔍 MOT Intelligence
MOT expiry dates are tracked for all stock. Vehicles expiring in under 90 days are flagged. The DVSA MOT History API can be called to get full history and advisories. Vehicles with repeat advisories are highlighted for pre-sale attention.
